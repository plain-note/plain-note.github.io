Privacy Policy

The software application PlainNote (“App”) is a simple plain text editor with no purpose other than creating and editing plain text files.

The App DOES NOT collect or transmit any kind of information about its users or the content they create or edit throught the App. The App HAS NO facility to access the Internet.

But... the App MAY indirectly collect and transmit app crash data (“Error Reporting Data”) solely for the purpose of testing and resolving compatibility issues, fixing and resolving “bugs” or other quality issues related to the App.

This policy is subject to change without notice. If you disagree with this policy, please discontinue use of the App.
